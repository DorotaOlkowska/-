Dobry Gucio!

Colour scheme: Black, M:100
Fonts: UTM Avo Regular/Bold
Signet: chihuahua

Pet shop

The first of my tasks in the creation of this pet shop was to come up with a concept for the name of the shop. As you can see, creativity has many sides :) I wanted the name of the shop to be out of the box and at the same time close to each customer. "Good Gucio!" is a reference to the name of the owner's dog and an oft-spoken phrase when the pet is polite.
The next step was to design a logo. The logo consists of the name of the shop and a signet created from a photography of the owner's dog. The unconventional shop name is combined with the striking colour of 100% magenta, which is intended to be an eye-catcher. It is worth noting that, depending on the form and colour of the graphic message, it is possible to change the 100% magenta colour in the logotype to another leading colour of the project in which the logo is used. This ensures that the logo still retains its character while avoiding the possibility of disrupting the colour scheme of a project.

Design Social Media

Each SM graphic consists of a short topic word with a 30° rotated exclamation mark on a solid background with a small photo element including an animal relating to the content of the post. An important element of the graphics is the strong colour on the background or text. Such a treatment is intended to catch the attention of the viewers and make them interested in the content. The concept was intended as a response to the muted pastel colours currently leading the way in design.

Photo studio

When I was reviewing most photography business logos, I came across the use of handwritten fonts with delicate elements. I realise that, this is due to the equation between photography and weddings. I was given a lot of confidence in this design, so I decided to go for a crisp, stronger design that stood out. The main font is sharp and angular, a reference to the shape of film from the first cameras. The secondary font is a counterbalance to the main font so that the final logo design is not too heavy for the viewer. The colour scheme is navy blue and white, making it easy to match and apply to photos as a watermark.
2022 Dorota Olkowska
